About Paul Whitson

For over 30 years, Paul Whitson has provided excellent piano tuning services, as well as music lessons and affordable in-home instruction. With a Masters in Music Education and decades of classroom and private teaching experience, Paul works with students of all ages and pianos of all types.

Paul specializes in gentle, encouraging teaching methods and careful piano voicing and tuning. Lessons include sight-reading, ear training, scales, and performance preparation.
